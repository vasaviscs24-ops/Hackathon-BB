Phase Shift

Annual International Technical Symposium of BMSCE

What is Phase Shift?

Phase Shift is an Annual International Technical Symposium organized by B.M.S. College of Engineering that brings together individuals from diverse fields to explore and advance technology and innovation. The event fosters curiosity, a passion for learning, and the exchange of knowledge and skills, aiming to shape professionals capable of competing on a global stage.

It was originally started as a celebration of Engineer's Day, to commemorate the birth of India's greatest Engineer, Sir M. Visvesvaraya, and over the years has evolved into a dynamic conduit for substantial industry interaction.

Edition History & Themes

  • 2024 (10th Edition) — "Resurgence: Transforming Tomorrow Sustainably"
    Held on 5th and 6th December 2024. The theme emphasized revitalizing past innovations to build a more sustainable and eco-friendly future, with projects mapped to UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • 2023 (9th Edition) — "Techtopia: Into the Future"
    Conducted on 14th and 15th December 2023. Powered by Augmented Reality, it integrated virtual and physical realms, exploring AI, quantum computing, 5G, blockchain, and cybersecurity.
  • 2022 (8th Edition) — "Datafication and Digital Trust"
    Conducted on 25th and 26th November 2022, addressing the data revolution in a post-pandemic world, with a focus on digital privacy, security, and trust.

Structure of the Event

Inauguration Featuring prominent industry leaders as Chief Guests and Guests of Honour from companies like Intel AI, Bosch India, Accenture, TerraBlue XT, and EMC Engineers.
Project Display Developing projects aligned with UN SDGs. Outstanding projects are recognized through awards including Maximum Participant Award & Most SDGs Mapping.
Pre-Phase Shift Events Warm-up events leading up to the main fest: seminars, health awareness sessions, sustainability runs, ideathons, and guest talks.
Central Events High-visibility events like Pragathi, LiveWire, Hackaphasia, Hardware Hackathon, and 24-Hour Codeathon.

Scale & Impact

  • The event spans all departments and clubs of BMSCE — from Aerospace and Biotechnology to AI/ML, Civil, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering.
  • Participants come from across the globe, fostering a collaborative atmosphere for innovation.
  • Industry judges and resource persons are brought in from top companies including HPE, Amazon, Nutanix, C-DAC, Zerodha, Mitsubishi Electric, MongoDB, and more.
  • Phase Shift 2024 reflected its commitment to sustainability through eco-friendly initiatives such as the use of 100% recyclable materials.
